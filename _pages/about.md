Hi, I am a rising senior studying Computer Engineering at **Virginia Tech**, with a focus on Control, Robotics and Autonomy (CRA). I am an Undergraduate Research Intern in the HAIM Lab at the Virginia Tech Grado Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, advised by Dr. Yuhao Zhong. My current work explores medical robotics through 3D-printing robotics simulation with Python and Isaac Sim.

Previously, I studied Electrical and Computer Engineering at **Sichuan University** in Chengdu, China. My research experience includes infrared-thermography diagnosis of pancreatic cancer with Dr. Jeungphill Hanne, medical image-based fracture diagnosis, and fNIRS-based classification for Alzheimer's disease with Dr. Zheng Yang.

My technical interests include industrial robotics, simulation, medical robotics, C++, Python, Isaac Sim, MATLAB, Linux, and 3D printing.
